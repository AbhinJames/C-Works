How can I pick out the right size for my kitchen when shopping for countertop microwaves? A home without counter space can easily accommodate a larger microwave if you bring in a portable kitchen island, or a microwave or utility cart for easy placement and then storage out of the way. Microwaves have an interior capacity that ranges from less than a cubic foot all the way up to two cubic feet or more. It is all a matter of measuring your counter space.
