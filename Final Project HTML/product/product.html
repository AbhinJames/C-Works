20.43" 1.1 cu. ft. Countertop Microwave

Superior performance, practicality, and dependability are what stands out with this top-quality mid-sized microwave oven by Danby. The durable, attractive stainless-steel interior and exterior finish make it the perfect appliance to combine with any style of existing room décor.

It comes with 10 unique power-level choices to make sure your favourite foods are cooked to perfection.
